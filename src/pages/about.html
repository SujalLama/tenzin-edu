About Us

Tenzin Education Consultancy and Placement Firm (ECPF) is a registered Education Consultancy and Placement Firm under the Department of Adult and Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Bhutan.

We have been in operation since 2017 and have been offering reliable and integrated services solutions to the prospective students of Bhutan who wishes to pursue their further studies in Australia. Our office is managed by well trained and Certified Education Counsellors, and helpful staff to assist prospective students with their enrolment and visa processing. Since inception, we have been a successful agent which we were able to help more than a hundred students to enrol in various study fields in Australia.

At Tenzin ECPF, students are more than just a collection of business transactions. Once a student comes on board as a registered client, they become part of our family, and utmost importance is placed in attending to their every individual need. This reciprocity is clearly demonstrated by our loyal students who in turn recommend our services to their family and friends.

Our team of expertly trained professionals comes from a diverse array of educational backgrounds; this gives them a depth and breadth of understanding from which to carefully assess a student’s situation, various needs and ambitions and to provide them with the best option for their admission and student visa application procedures. We are a dedicated and professional team of Qualified Education Agent Counsellors with a combined expertise of years.

We aim to be as comprehensive as possible in our approach. To this end, we ensure that students who have been accepted to study in Australia are thoroughly prepared and know what exactly is expected of them. This guidance and support are provided through programs such as the pre-departure orientation, where we educate and sensitize prospective students on what to expect upon reaching Australia. After their arrival, we offer to continue, on-going support such as help with obtaining accommodation and advising on job prospects.

Tenzin ECPF is a client focused and result driven education consultancy firm in Bhutan that provides complete services as follows. We ensure that we provide best services to meet our client’s expectations whenever they chose our services. We also ensure that our services are guided by our values and professional ethics.
